What is Vastu Shastra ?
Vastu Shastra is a traditional Indian system of architecture and design. Vastushastra is considered to be a comprehensive and holistic approach to design and construction, incorporating principles of architecture, astrology, and spirituality. The aim of Vastushastra is to create living spaces that are in harmony with the natural environment and promote physical, mental, and spiritual well-being. According to Vastushastra, the placement of rooms, doors, windows, and furniture in a house or building should be carefully planned to ensure optimal energy flow and balance.

According to Vastushastra, every building should be constructed in a way that maximizes positive energy flow and minimizes negative influences. The orientation of the building, placement of rooms, and even the color of walls are all considered to have an impact on the well-being and prosperity of the inhabitants.
